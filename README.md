# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: August 12, 2025_

## Introduction

Welcome to Scroll Rocket ("we," "our," or "us"). This extension is developed with passion and love by Ä°lker Binzet, not for commercial purposes but to improve user experience on the web. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our browser extension.

## Information We Collect

### User Settings

- Button position preferences (bottom-right, bottom-left, top-right, top-left)
- Button size customization (20-60px)
- Button color preferences
- Distance from edge settings (10-100px)
- Exception rules for specific websites
- Language preference (English/Turkish)
- Theme preferences (light/dark mode)

This information is stored locally on your device and synced through your browser account if you enable browser sync.

### Usage Data

We collect NO usage data, analytics, or telemetry. This extension operates completely offline after installation.

### What We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect:

- Personal identification information
- Browsing history
- Website content
- Form data
- Passwords
- Credit card information
- Any personally identifiable information
- Analytics or usage statistics
- Telemetry data

## How We Use Your Information

### User Settings

- To provide customized scroll-to-top button experience
- To sync your preferences across devices (if browser sync enabled)
- To restore your settings after updates
- To remember your exception rules for specific websites
- To display the interface in your preferred language

### No Data Collection

Since we don't collect usage data, we don't use it for anything. Your settings stay on your device.

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

- User settings are stored locally on your device
- Settings are encrypted using standard Chrome storage APIs
- Data is only accessible by the Scroll Rocket extension

### Browser Sync

- If enabled, your settings sync across devices through your browser
- Sync data is encrypted by your browser (Chrome/Edge)
- We never have access to your browser sync data
- Extension works completely offline

## Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with:

- Third-party services
- Advertisers
- Marketing companies
- Data brokers
- Any other external parties

## Browser Permissions

Our extension requires minimal permissions:

- **Storage**: To save your settings locally
- **ActiveTab**: To add the scroll button to the current page only
- **Tabs (Optional)**: Only requested when you use "Use current site" feature
- **Host permissions**: To work on http and https websites

We do NOT request:

- Access to all websites permanently
- Reading/modifying webpage content
- Access to downloads, bookmarks, or browsing history
- Camera, microphone, or location permissions
